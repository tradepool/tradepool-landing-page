      Features

      Verified

      No more bots and pumpers. See who actually has skin in the game and how they have performed before you choose to put any consideration in their opinion

      Crowdsourced

      Days of manually trying to gauge sentiment around particular stocks are over. See how often a publicly traded asset is being mentioned and traded by other users. Bullish or Bearish flags are required for every post and then aggregated along with actual trades being placed for easy to access, real-time sentiment

      Inclusive

      Interact with and see the verified trades, holdings, and performance of other users - regardless of the brokerage platforms being used. You dont need to open a new brokerage account to participate in social trading.

      Competitive

      Our first of its kind Leaderboard shows the top trades over various time periods. This is in terms of realized trades and in terms of total portfolio value increases.

      Anonymous

      Tradepool never shares information such as your real name or how much money you have. Everything (trades, holdings, performance) is in terms of percentages, never dollars. Share as much personal information as youd like or none at all.

      Terms Editor

      Terms and privacy policies are complex enough, creating pages for them shouldn't be. We have made it as easy as using a word processor to create these pages.

      What is Tradepool?

      Tradepool is the one-stop-shop that modern day retail traders never knew they needed. We recognize that many people use social media to some extent to get new ideas on what to trade or to gauge sentiment on specific tickers. We simplify and strengthen this process by giving users a transparent look-through on what people are talking about, what they are actually trading, and how they have performed. See what your friends are trading, get in on the next hot stock, and join a community of verified traders of your favorite stock - all with Tradepool.
